# Noosphere City Gazipaşa

**Status: proposed pilot / draft.** This repository describes a proposed AI founder residency and civic innovation lab for Gazipaşa, Türkiye. Nothing here is approved by any municipality, developer, or partner yet. All claims about housing, partners, and timelines should be read as a working proposal.

## The idea in one paragraph

Noosphere City Gazipaşa is an AI founder residency where local partners provide temporary housing, workspace, and access to real local challenges. In exchange, selected AI and startup teams build practical pilots for tourism, agriculture, real estate transparency, education, environmental monitoring, smart city services, and local business growth. The goal is not another isolated startup campus. The goal is to turn Gazipaşa into a living laboratory where founders improve the city while building globally useful AI products.

## Why Gazipaşa

- A Mediterranean district roughly 40 km east of Alanya with its own airport (Gazipaşa-Alanya).
- A real agricultural base: greenhouses, bananas, citrus, avocado, tropical fruit.
- Growing tourism and real estate interest, but not as overheated as Alanya or Antalya.
- Quiet, walkable, family-friendly, with sea, mountains, ancient sites, and protected nature (Caretta Caretta).
- Room for a differentiated identity: not just another resort, but an AI + ecology + agriculture + tourism living lab.

## The exchange

**Founders receive:**
- Temporary housing (1–3 months).
- A shared workspace.
- A small founder community.
- Access to real local challenges and stakeholders.
- A demo day and a public report.

**The city and partners receive:**
- Working pilots tied to local problems.
- Open documentation and code where possible.
- Public workshops for residents and children.
- International visibility for Gazipaşa as an emerging tech-friendly district.

## Pilot shape

A 90-day pilot with 3–5 apartments or apart-hotel rooms, 5–10 selected builders, and 3–5 local challenge tracks. Weekly build reviews, a final demo day, and a public report.

## What this is not

- Not a request for free apartments forever.
- Not a claim of municipal approval.
- Not a promise of guaranteed funding or visas.
- Not a hype manifesto.

It is a working pilot proposal that anyone — a municipality officer, a developer, a hotel owner, a farmer cooperative, a founder — can read in one sitting and decide whether to talk further.

## Economic principle

Economy, in its living sense, is a fair exchange of value between participants — one that each side considers honest. Money only measures part of that exchange.

The pilot should only proceed if each participant can clearly answer: *What do I give? What do I receive? Why is this fair?*

Housing support, local challenges, founder work, public visibility, prototypes, and partner access are not charity or extraction. They form a transparent exchange where all sides benefit. No participant should be treated as a resource without a return of value.
