# Gazipaşa Pilot — Overview

**Status: proposed / draft.**

This document describes the proposed first pilot of Noosphere City in Gazipaşa, Türkiye. It is a starting point for conversations with the municipality, local developers, hotels, real estate agencies, schools, farmers, and tourism businesses.

## Context

Gazipaşa is a coastal district in Antalya Province, roughly 40 km east of Alanya, with its own airport (Gazipaşa-Alanya). It combines:

- A working agricultural economy (greenhouses, bananas, citrus, avocado, tropical fruit).
- Growing but still moderate tourism and real estate activity.
- Protected nature, beaches, ancient sites, and Caretta Caretta nesting areas.
- Reasonable cost of living compared to Antalya and Alanya.

Gazipaşa has the ingredients to host a small, focused AI residency without the cost or noise of larger tech hubs.

## Pilot summary

- **Duration:** 90 days.
- **Housing:** 3–5 apartments or apart-hotel rooms, ideally during low season.
- **Builders:** 5–10 selected founders or small teams.
- **Tracks:** 3–5 local challenge tracks (see `city_challenge_catalog.md`).
- **Cadence:** weekly build reviews, biweekly community sessions, one final demo day.
- **Outputs:** working prototypes, public report, GitHub artifacts, at least one workshop for residents.

## Goals of the pilot

1. Prove that founders can do useful technical work for Gazipaşa in 90 days.
2. Test whether local partners (developers, hotels, agencies, municipality) find the exchange worthwhile.
3. Produce concrete, public artifacts the next cohort can build on.
4. Identify which tracks deserve a longer-term program.

## What the pilot is **not**

- Not a generic accelerator.
- Not a real estate sales channel.
- Not a tourism marketing campaign.
- Not a closed corporate R&D lab.

## Success criteria

- At least 3 tracks produce working prototypes demoed publicly.
- At least 1 track produces a pilot adopted or seriously evaluated by a local stakeholder.
- At least 1 free public workshop for residents or children.
- A written, public 90-day report.
- Clear answer to the question: should Noosphere City run a second cohort in Gazipaşa?

## Open questions

- Which partner provides the first 3 housing units?
- Who hosts the workspace?
- Which 3 tracks are chosen for the first cohort?
- What is the legal vehicle (association, cooperation with a technopark, MoU with the municipality)?
- How are founders selected and on what visa basis do they enter Türkiye?

These questions are intentionally open. The pilot proposal is a starting position, not a finished plan.
