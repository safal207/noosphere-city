# Pilot Budget Scenarios

**Status: proposed / draft.**

This document gives rough budget scenarios for a first Noosphere City Gazipaşa pilot. These are estimates, not commitments. Actual costs depend on housing partners, season, legal structure, participant count, workspace, insurance, translation, and local operations.

## Budget principle

The first pilot should be cheap enough to fail safely and useful enough to prove whether the model deserves expansion.

## Scenario A — Lean pilot

- 3–5 founders or builders
- 30–45 days
- partner-supported housing or discounted rooms
- lightweight coordination
- one local demo day
- estimated external budget: **€5,000–€10,000**

Budget categories:

- coordination,
- basic workspace and events,
- local transport,
- translation and admin,
- demo day,
- documentation and reporting,
- basic legal review allowance,
- emergency reserve.

## Scenario B — Standard pilot

- 5–10 founders or builders
- 60–90 days
- partial housing support
- part-time operator and coordinator
- structured weekly reviews
- final public demo day and report
- estimated external budget: **€15,000–€30,000**

Budget categories:

- housing subsidy,
- operator and coordination,
- legal and accounting review,
- workspace and events,
- demo day,
- marketing and content,
- local challenge support,
- documentation and reporting,
- emergency reserve.

## Scenario C — Strong pilot

- 10–15 founders or builders
- 90 days
- dedicated operator
- stronger partner support
- more formal legal and operational structure
- estimated external budget: **€40,000–€75,000**

Budget categories:

- housing support,
- operating team,
- legal and accounting review,
- workspace and events,
- translation and admin,
- insurance and liability review,
- demo day,
- content, media, and reporting,
- challenge owner support,
- emergency reserve.

## What is intentionally excluded

- long-term salaries,
- guaranteed relocation support,
- visa sponsorship,
- investment into resident startups,
- permanent office lease,
- token launch,
- large marketing campaigns,
- guaranteed municipal spending,
- large-scale infrastructure development.

## Funding mix to test

Possible mix:

- housing partner in-kind contribution,
- local sponsor,
- small grant,
- paid seats,
- demo day sponsorship,
- corporate challenge fee,
- founder contribution for optional services.

The pilot should not depend on a single funding source.

## Economic validation metrics

- cost per resident,
- cost per prototype,
- number of prototypes completed,
- number of local partners engaged,
- number of pilots continued after demo day,
- partner satisfaction,
- resident satisfaction,
- number of paid continuation opportunities,
- public artifacts produced.
