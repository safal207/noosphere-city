# Legal and Operational Scope

**Status: proposed / draft.**

This document records the legal and operational boundaries of Noosphere City Gazipaşa as it exists today. It is intended to prevent misunderstandings with founders, partners, and public institutions.

## What this repository is

- A draft proposal for an AI founder residency and civic innovation lab.
- A working set of ideas, frameworks, and templates open for review and revision.

## What this repository is **not**

- Not a legal entity.
- Not an association, company, foundation, or registered NGO.
- Not an approved municipal program.
- Not an immigration, visa, or relocation service.
- Not a real estate listing, brokerage, or housing platform.
- Not a source of legal, tax, employment, or financial advice.

## Specific disclaimers

- **No municipal approval is claimed.** Mentions of the Gazipaşa Municipality describe a desired partner, not a confirmed one.
- **No housing commitments are claimed.** Any reference to apartments, hotels, or apart-hotels describes possible partners, not signed agreements.
- **No visa or work permit guidance is provided.** Founders are responsible for their own immigration status, visas, work permits, residency permits, and tax obligations in Türkiye and their home countries.
- **No employment relationship is created.** Participation in the residency does not imply employment, contractor status, or any payment obligation by either side, unless a separate written agreement says so.
- **No investment is offered or solicited.** This repository does not constitute an offer of securities, an investment opportunity, or a fundraising vehicle.

## What a real pilot would require

Before any actual cohort is run on the ground in Gazipaşa, the following must be in place:

- A local legal review of the chosen operating structure.
- Written agreements with each housing or workspace partner, reviewed by qualified counsel.
- Written terms for participants covering selection, conduct, deliverables, intellectual property, privacy, and termination.
- Clarity on the legal vehicle (for example: an association, a Turkish company, a foundation, a municipality cooperation agreement, or a technopark partnership).
- Clarity on the visa basis under which each participant enters and stays in Türkiye.
- Compliance with local tax, labor, data protection, and tourism-housing regulations.
- Insurance arrangements appropriate to the activity and venue.

Until these are in place, references in this repository to "the residency" describe an intention, not an operating program.

## Promises we will not make until formal agreements exist

- No promise of free housing.
- No promise of a visa, work permit, or residency permit.
- No promise of employment or paid contract work.
- No promise of investment, grants, or funding.
- No promise of relocation support beyond information.

If any document or communication implies such a promise without a signed agreement, that document is wrong and should be corrected.

## Future structural options under consideration

The project may later explore one or more of the following, each with its own legal and tax implications:

- A Turkish association (dernek).
- A Turkish company.
- A foundation (vakıf).
- A formal cooperation agreement with the Gazipaşa Municipality.
- A technopark partnership (for example via Türkiye Tech Visa or a Turkish technopark).
- An incubator or accelerator partnership.

The choice between these options is open and will depend on partners, funding, and legal advice at the time.

## How to read this repository

- Treat every concrete number (units, residents, days) as a placeholder.
- Treat every named partner type as a candidate, not a confirmed party.
- Treat every plan as a draft until a corresponding signed document exists.
