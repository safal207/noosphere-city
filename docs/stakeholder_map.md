# Stakeholder Map

**Status: proposed / draft.**

Noosphere City Gazipaşa only works if the right local and international stakeholders see a clear reason to participate. This map outlines who they are and what each side can realistically offer and ask.

## Local stakeholders

### Gazipaşa Municipality
- **Can offer:** public legitimacy, access to city challenges, a meeting venue, local media reach, introductions to district businesses.
- **Wants:** practical improvements for residents, positive visibility for the district, low-risk pilots, no unfunded obligations.
- **First ask:** an MoU for a 90-day pilot, not funding.

### Local developers and construction companies
- **Can offer:** unsold or seasonally vacant apartments, small office space.
- **Wants:** PR, international visibility, association with a forward-looking initiative, future buyers and tenants.
- **First ask:** 2–3 units during low season in exchange for branded participation.

### Hotels and apart-hotels
- **Can offer:** rooms during low season at reduced or sponsored rates, meeting space, hospitality services.
- **Wants:** off-season occupancy, content, association with a respected program.
- **First ask:** a low-season rate or a small donated allocation for the first cohort.

### Real estate agencies
- **Can offer:** market knowledge, intro to landlords, due diligence support.
- **Wants:** reputation as trustworthy, access to international clients, alignment with transparency efforts.
- **First ask:** advisory role on the real estate transparency track.

### Farmers and agricultural cooperatives
- **Can offer:** real agricultural problems, field access, domain knowledge.
- **Wants:** practical tools that improve yield, reduce loss, and open new markets.
- **First ask:** willingness to host one or two field visits and review prototypes.

### Schools and youth groups
- **Can offer:** learners, venues, parent networks.
- **Wants:** modern AI and coding exposure for children, free workshops.
- **First ask:** host one or two free workshops per cohort.

### Tourism businesses (restaurants, tour operators, marina, dive shops)
- **Can offer:** real customer flows, local data, content.
- **Wants:** more visibility, better customer experience, off-season demand.
- **First ask:** willingness to pilot one tool from the cohort.

## International stakeholders

### Founders and small AI teams
- **Can offer:** technical work, time, public artifacts.
- **Wants:** focus, affordability, community, real problems, visibility.
- **First ask:** apply, commit to deliverables, respect the place.

### Türkiye Tech Visa and technoparks
- **Can offer:** formal R&D status, technopark partnerships, visa and consultancy support for qualifying founders.
- **Wants:** credible talent pipelines and serious projects.
- **First ask:** information sessions and a referral pathway for selected residents.

### Diaspora and international press
- **Can offer:** storytelling, audience, credibility.
- **Wants:** authentic, well-documented stories.
- **First ask:** access to demo day and the public report.

## Stakeholders we will not pursue in the pilot

- Speculative real estate investors looking only for short-term flips.
- Sponsors who require closed-source outputs without public benefit.
- Partners who demand exclusive control over the program or its narrative.

## Decision principle

If a stakeholder cannot articulate both what they offer and what they want from the residency, they are not yet a stakeholder. They are a contact.
