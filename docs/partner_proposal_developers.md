# Partner Proposal — Local Developers, Hotels, and Real Estate Agencies

**Status: proposed / draft. Not yet shared with any partner.**

This document is a starting proposal for private partners who can provide housing or workspace for the first Noosphere City Gazipaşa cohort.

## Summary

We propose a 90-day pilot AI founder residency in Gazipaşa. We need 3–5 housing units and, ideally, a shared workspace for low-season months. In exchange, the partner is named as a founding pilot partner, receives documented PR, and gets early access to a curated international founder community.

## Who this proposal is for

- Local developers with unsold or seasonally vacant inventory.
- Apart-hotels and hotels with low-season capacity.
- Real estate agencies that can coordinate landlords willing to host residents.
- Coworking or office space owners with underused capacity.

## What we ask

- 3–5 housing units for 1–3 months during low season, at sponsored or significantly reduced rates.
- Optionally, a shared workspace for the cohort (10–15 people, basic infrastructure).
- A named contact responsible for handover, maintenance, and check-out.

## What partners receive

- Status as a founding pilot partner of Noosphere City Gazipaşa.
- Mention in the public 90-day report and demo day.
- Branded coverage in cohort communications and on the residency website.
- Access to demo day and to the cohort community.
- A documented case study they can use in their own marketing.
- An introduction channel to founders who may become future tenants, buyers, or business partners.

## What we do **not** promise

- No guaranteed sales or rentals beyond the pilot.
- No exclusive marketing rights.
- No claims that the partner is endorsed by the municipality.
- No commitments to renew beyond the 90-day pilot without a joint review.

## Standards we ask partners to accept

- Honest information about housing conditions and costs.
- Respect for residents' privacy and working time.
- A simple written agreement covering term, condition, and termination.
- Willingness to be named publicly in the cohort report.

## Suggested first conversation

A short meeting to:

1. Walk through the residency model.
2. Confirm available units and timing.
3. Agree on a simple MoU template.
4. Define a single point of contact on each side.

## Outcome we are looking for

By the end of the first conversation, both sides should be able to answer: *can we run a 90-day pilot together, starting in the next low season, with a clear and limited scope?*
