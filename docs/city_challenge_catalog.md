# City Challenge Catalog

**Status: proposed / draft.**

This catalog lists candidate tracks for the first Noosphere City Gazipaşa cohort. The first pilot will run only 3–5 of these; the rest are kept here for later cohorts or partner conversations. Tracks are intentionally narrow so that founders can ship in 90 days.

## Track 1 — AI Tourism Guide

**Problem.** Visitors to Gazipaşa rely on generic platforms that do not surface local hidden gems, off-season options, or practical tips.

**Possible deliverables.**
- A multilingual AI guide focused on Gazipaşa and surrounding villages.
- Curated routes: beaches, ancient sites, farms, mountain trails, marina.
- A simple recommendation engine for local businesses.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Tour operators, marina, hotels, the municipality.

## Track 2 — Agriculture and Greenhouse Intelligence

**Problem.** Local farmers and greenhouse operators lack accessible tools for plant diagnosis, yield tracking, and price decisions.

**Possible deliverables.**
- Plant disease and pest diagnosis from a phone photo.
- Greenhouse condition monitoring and alerts.
- A simple harvest and pricing assistant for small farms.
- A pilot marketplace from farmers to local restaurants and hotels.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Farmer cooperatives, individual greenhouses, restaurants, hotel kitchens.

## Track 3 — Real Estate Transparency

**Problem.** International buyers and renters often face opaque listings, inconsistent pricing, and limited due diligence support.

**Possible deliverables.**
- Neighborhood transparency dashboard for Gazipaşa.
- A rental yield and total-cost calculator.
- A due diligence checklist for foreign buyers.
- A scam-pattern detector for listings.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Real estate agencies willing to commit to transparency standards.

## Track 4 — Local Business Discovery

**Problem.** Small local businesses struggle to reach visitors and new residents beyond word of mouth.

**Possible deliverables.**
- A clean, ad-free local business map.
- A multilingual menu and service translator.
- A lightweight reservation or contact tool for small operators.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Restaurants, cafes, small shops, service providers.

## Track 5 — Smart City Issue Reporting

**Problem.** Residents have no easy way to report and track local issues such as broken lighting, waste, road damage, or accessibility problems.

**Possible deliverables.**
- A simple issue reporting app with photo and location.
- A dashboard for the municipality to see categorized issues.
- An AI-assisted triage and routing layer.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Municipality, neighborhood associations.

## Track 6 — Environmental Monitoring

**Problem.** Coastal and natural areas around Gazipaşa, including Caretta Caretta nesting sites, need lightweight monitoring tools and public awareness.

**Possible deliverables.**
- A clean coast reporting tool.
- A Caretta Caretta awareness and nesting-season app.
- Eco-tourism route planning with environmental impact notes.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Environmental groups, the municipality, tour operators.

## Track 7 — Education and AI Workshops for Children

**Problem.** Local children have limited exposure to modern AI and coding outside of large cities.

**Possible deliverables.**
- Free weekend AI and coding workshops.
- A small open curriculum for local teachers.
- A summer program plan for the next cohort.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Schools, youth groups, parent associations.

## Track 8 — Mobility and Parking

**Problem.** Seasonal traffic, parking pressure, and transport links to the airport and surrounding villages create friction for residents and visitors.

**Possible deliverables.**
- Parking and traffic pattern analysis using public data and simple sensors.
- A simple shared mobility or shuttle planning prototype.
- An open simulation of seasonal flows.

**Pilot partner candidates.** Municipality, transport operators, marina.

## Selection rules for the first cohort

- Pick no more than 5 tracks.
- Each chosen track must have at least one named local stakeholder willing to engage.
- Each chosen track must be shippable in 90 days by 1–3 people.
- At least one track must directly serve residents (not only tourists or buyers).
