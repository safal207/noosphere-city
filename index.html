Noosphere City
Gazipaşa

A proposed AI founder residency and civic innovation lab for Gazipaşa, Türkiye. Founders build real prototypes. Local partners provide housing, venues, and real challenges. Everyone gets something fair in return.

I'm interested as a founder I'm interested as a local partner Read the pilot plan

Residency-for-impact model

Noosphere City is not "free apartments for startups." It is a fair value exchange where every participant contributes something real and receives something real in return.

What local partners provide

  • Temporary housing or venue access
  • Real local challenges and data access
  • Local introductions and context
  • Visibility as an innovation-friendly host

What founders provide

  • Working prototypes for local problems
  • Public workshops and documentation
  • Demo-day outputs and open code
  • International visibility for Gazipaşa

The pilot should only proceed if each participant can clearly answer: What do I contribute? What do I receive? Why is this fair?

Pilot structure

Two formats are proposed, both requiring at least one confirmed anchor partner before launch.

Option A

Lean pilot

  • 3–5 founders / builders
  • 30–45 days on the ground
  • 2–3 challenge tracks
  • 2 working prototypes at demo day
  • 1 public report

Suitable if only one housing partner is confirmed.

Option B

Standard pilot

  • 5–10 founders / builders
  • 60–90 days on the ground
  • 3–5 challenge tracks
  • 3–5 working prototypes at demo day
  • 1 public demo day event
  • 1 public report + continuation path

Requires 2–3 partners and confirmed challenge owners.

Challenge tracks

Tracks are proposed based on Gazipaşa's real assets: agriculture, tourism, real estate, education, and environment. Each track is tied to a local challenge owner — a farm, hotel, school, or business with a real problem to solve.

🗺️

AI tourism guide

Contextual, honest recommendations for Gazipaşa's food, nature, history, and crafts.

🌿

Agriculture intelligence

Greenhouse monitoring, harvest prediction, and input optimization for local farmers.

🏡

Real estate transparency

Price history, zoning context, and honest market data for buyers and developers.

🛍️

Local business discovery

Searchable, up-to-date directory of local producers, restaurants, artisans, and services.

📣

Smart city issue reporting

Lightweight tool for residents to report infrastructure problems with follow-up tracking.

🌊

Environmental monitoring

Coastal, air, or soil data collection for protected areas including Caretta Caretta habitat.

🎓

Education & AI workshops

Free public AI literacy workshops for children, teachers, and local businesses.

Who can partner

Each partner type contributes something specific and receives something specific.

Hotels / Apart-hotels

Housing partner

  • Give: 3–10 rooms for 30–90 days
  • Receive: AI tourism prototype, international press, demo-day visibility, pilot-partner credit
Developers / Real Estate

Venue partner

  • Give: Apartments or commercial space
  • Receive: Real estate transparency prototype, branded visibility, early adopter positioning
Municipality / Institutions

Civic partner

  • Give: Challenge access, introductions, non-binding support letter
  • Receive: Smart city prototypes, public report, pilot proof of concept
Local Businesses / Farms

Challenge owner

  • Give: Real problem + data access + feedback
  • Receive: Working prototype tailored to their problem, no cost
Founders / Builders

Resident

  • Give: Prototype, workshop, documentation, demo
  • Receive: Housing support, local access, community, demo day, public report credit
Mentors / Supporters

Knowledge partner

  • Give: Expertise, advisory time, network introductions
  • Receive: Early access to prototypes, public acknowledgment, access to pilot findings

Legal and scope note

This is a draft proposal

Noosphere City Gazipaşa is a draft proposal. It is not a legal entity, does not sponsor visas, does not promise housing, and has no confirmed municipal approval or partners yet. Any real pilot requires local legal review and written partner agreements.

No employment is offered or implied. No investment is solicited. No tokens, credits, or speculative instruments are part of this pilot. Prototypes produced during the pilot are not production-ready systems. Any collection of personal data requires review under Turkish KVKK and, where relevant, GDPR.
