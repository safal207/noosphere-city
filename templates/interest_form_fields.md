# Interest Form Fields

**Status: draft specification. This document does not collect data by itself.**

This file defines the fields that would appear in a live interest form for Noosphere City Gazipaşa.
Until a form is deployed, interested parties should email safal0645@gmail.com and mention their role.

---

## Founder / builder

- Name (or alias)
- Email address
- Country of residence
- Primary skills (e.g. AI/ML, full-stack, data, design, product)
- Project idea or area of focus
- Preferred challenge track (see city_challenge_catalog.md)
- Earliest available start date
- Preferred duration (30, 45, 60, or 90 days)
- What they plan to build in 30–90 days (one paragraph)
- What support they need (housing only, workspace, mentorship, local introductions)
- GitHub, portfolio, or personal site

---

## Hotel / apart-hotel partner

- Organization name
- Contact person name and title
- Email address
- Location (neighborhood or district)
- Available rooms or apartments (approximate number)
- Seasonality constraints (e.g. available off-season only)
- Possible support forms (rooms at reduced rate, free for pilot period, workspace access)
- What they hope to receive in return
- Any prior collaboration with startups, NGOs, or public programs

---

## Developer / real estate partner

- Organization name
- Contact person name and title
- Email address
- Type of property available (apartments, commercial space, co-working)
- Number of units or approximate capacity
- Location
- Interest in pilot duration (30–90 days)
- Branding or visibility goals
- Openness to prototype use in their properties

---

## Local business / challenge owner

- Organization name
- Contact person name and title
- Email address
- Type of business (farm, restaurant, shop, school, cooperative, etc.)
- Problem or challenge to solve (one paragraph)
- Data or resources available to founders working on the challenge
- Expected outcome from a pilot prototype
- Feedback availability (willing to test and provide feedback)

---

## Municipality / public institution

- Department name
- Contact person name and title
- Email address
- Local challenge areas of interest (infrastructure, tourism, environment, education, etc.)
- Possible forms of support (introductions, letter of support, venue access, data sharing)
- Public communication constraints (what may or may not be published)
- Decision-making process for non-binding cooperation

---

## Mentor / supporter

- Name
- Email
- Domain expertise
- Availability (hours per month, remote or in-person)
- Specific interest (a particular challenge track, general advisory, network introductions)
- Any relevant prior projects or affiliations

---

*This is a draft form specification. Actual data collection requires a deployed form and a privacy policy reviewed under applicable data protection rules including Turkish KVKK and, where relevant, GDPR.*
