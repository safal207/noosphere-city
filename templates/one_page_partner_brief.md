# Noosphere City Gazipaşa — One-Page Partner Brief

**Project name:** Noosphere City Gazipaşa
**Status:** Draft proposal. No municipal approval, no confirmed partners, no funded program.

## Core idea

A small AI founder residency in Gazipaşa, Türkiye. International founders spend 30–90 days in the district, working on real local challenges in tourism, agriculture, real estate transparency, education, environmental monitoring, and local business growth. The goal is a living laboratory where founders improve the city while building globally useful AI products.

## Pilot model

- **Lean pilot:** 30–45 days, 3–5 founders, 1 anchor partner, 2–3 challenge tracks, 1 demo day, 1 public report.
- **Standard pilot:** 60–90 days, 5–10 founders, 2–3 partners, 3–5 challenge tracks, 1 demo day, 1 public report.

## What partners provide

One or more of:

- low-season housing (apart-hotel rooms or unused apartments),
- a venue for workshops or a demo day,
- a real local challenge to prototype around,
- introductions to local stakeholders,
- review of public communication.

## What partners receive

- visibility as a co-host of a civic innovation pilot,
- demo-day presence and a public report mention,
- association with international AI / startup founders,
- open documentation of the pilot process,
- workshops for residents and children where appropriate.

## What is not claimed

- no municipal approval,
- no confirmed housing,
- no visa, work permit, tax, or employment guarantees,
- no investment commitments,
- no exclusive contracts.

A real pilot proceeds only after local legal review and a written, non-binding pilot outline with each partner.
